Working of Q-Cat

Connect to Q-CaT CPR Kit and receive learning analytics of CPR performed during or after training. Q-CaT CPR is an educational app served as a companion app for Q-CaT CPR Kit. The app is divided into three different sections which are

1. Q-CaT Kit Connection: Q-CaT CPR App connects Q-CaT CPR Kit, a physical kit to gauge the quality of CPR that is being performed. Once connected, insightful training analytics can be saved to improve the performance of CPR.
2. Emergency Section:It can serve to train public on what are the steps/ procedures that have to be taken during emergencies. The procedures are divided into three types which are segregated to child, adult & infant.
3. Learning Section: It comes with learning resources which explain glossa​ry, detailed explanation about CPR

EMERGENCY

We live and breathe our values

CPR(cardiopulmonaryresuscitation), is a life-saving technique used when a person's heart or breathing stops. In CPR, chest compressions and rescue breaths are given to keep the blood circulating and oxygen flowing through the body. CPR can be performed by anyone with the proper training, and can greatly increase the chances of survival in an emergency situation.

The importance of CPR cannot be overstated. In many cases, it is the difference between life and death. Prompt initiation of CPR can prevent brain damage and other serious complications that can occur when the heart or breathing stops. Knowing CPR can also give individuals the confidence and ability to act in an emergency situation, potentially saving the life of a loved one or even a stranger.

Learning CPR is a relatively simple and straightforward process, and training is widely available through organizations such as the American Heart Association and the Red Cross. By taking a CPR course and becoming certified, individuals can be prepared to respond to a cardiac or respiratory emergency, and make a significant impact on the outcome of the situation.
